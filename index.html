Bio:

“Sebastian Huergo has degrees both as a Film Director and Professor, however, is in music where he finds his main inspiration. That is why he has such a rhythmic and melodic approach to filming and video editing.

After more than 10 years of experience, translating ideas into audiovisual language has become his specialty, and what makes him stand out is the way he adds planning' to the creative process.
His main method is to intertwine three things: the idea to be communicated, the production designs, and the communication campaign. This has earned him awards as Best International Director (Miami, USA) and Best Editing (Bilbao, Spain), in addition to 9 international nominations.
Last but not least, it’s clear to the eye that Sebastian truly loves filming and producing. That is why he displays such a deep bond with every project he is involved in, and going the extra mile is always included"
